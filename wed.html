About Us

Dreamz Wedding Planner is a full-service wedding planning and management company in India, offering bespoke services for lavish weddings in exotic locations. With over 10 years of experience, they tailor each wedding to their clients needs, treating them as a piece of art. They collaborate with skilled specialists in event planning, decoration, and design.

To achieve this level of excellence, we collaborate with a diverse team of over highly skilled specialists. These include both outsourced experts and in-house professionals who specialise in various fields such as event planning, decoration, design, and more. We pride ourselves on our professionalism, creativity, and commitment to delivering extraordinary results

Our Services

Full-Service Planning

For couples who want a seamless experience from start to finish, our Full Wedding Planning service includes everything. We assist you with every aspect of your wedding, from budget management, venue selection, vendor coordination, and design to timeline creation and day-of execution. We handle the logistics, so you can focus on the fun.

Day-of Coordination

Our Day-of Coordination service ensures that your wedding day unfolds without a hitch. We take care of all the logistics, so you and your loved ones can fully enjoy the celebration. From overseeing the ceremony to managing the reception timeline, we handle all the details, allowing you to be present in the moment.

Destination Weddings

Planning a wedding away from home? Our Destination Wedding service offers everything you need to plan your dream wedding in a faraway location. We work with local vendors, arrange travel logistics, and ensure that every detail is executed perfectly, making your destination wedding a stress-free and magical experience.
